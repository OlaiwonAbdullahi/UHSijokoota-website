Emerge 2ndAt The 2023 WEST AFRICA GAS PIPELINE COMPANY QUIZ COMPETITION
11-OCT-2023 10:30AM

At the just concluded 2023 west Africa Gas Pipeline Company Quiz Competition our school emerge as the 2nd runner up on 10-oct-2023. Our students, Olaiwon Abdullahi (SS3),Aderogba Adedamola(SSS2), And Oguniyi Precious(SSS2),represented us at the competition and took 2nd position. At the contest they were tested on the use of English, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Literature, and Current Affairs, and our student did excellently well by answering almost all question right. The student were gifted a school bag, a 256GB tablet and a certificate each and also a silver trophy and a commercial waste bin for the school. Our past student took third position, so we decided that ours should be different. we thank Almighty GOD for making us be among the winners, we also wish to thank the organizers of the competition, may Almighty GOD grant them their heart desires
